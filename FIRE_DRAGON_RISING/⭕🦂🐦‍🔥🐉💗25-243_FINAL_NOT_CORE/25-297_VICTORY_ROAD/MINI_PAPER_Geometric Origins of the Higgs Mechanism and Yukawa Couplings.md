$$S = \int d^4x \sum_i \left|\nabla E_i\right|^2 + V(E_1,...,E_n)$$

$$\lambda = \frac{1}{4}\left(\frac{\partial^2 E}{\partial r^2}\right)^2$$

$$m_n = \frac{\hbar}{c} \oint_S \left|\nabla P_n\right| \, dA$$
